KEY INSIGHTS

Business Observation
1.	Profit Margin are remarkably consistent across all region (17.1% - 17.4%) Suggesting stable pricing strategy 
2.	All four Quarters contribute almost equally (25% each) to annual profit no major seasonal slump.
3.	February is a consistent weak month nearly 34% Lower Profit than January May Warrant targeted promotional activity 
4.	Electronics dominates at 50% of total sales, making it most critical category to monitor and invest in.

CONCLUSION
This E-Commerce Sales analysis Project Successfully processed 3500 orders to deliver meaningful business insights through both EXCEL AND POWER BI. The data reveals a healthy and consistent business with a 17.3% Profit Margin across all Region’s and Categories. 
Key action points for the business based on this analysis:
1.	Invest further in Electronics and Camera Products – Highest Revenue drivers 
2.	Investigate February Sales drop and plan Targeted Promotions  for that month
3.	Focus Growth efforts on the North Region which Lags behind other Regions
